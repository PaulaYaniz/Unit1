### Given two integers, print TRUE if one of them is equal to 20 or the sum of both is equal to 20.

```.py
num_1 = int(input("Enter a number: "))
num_2 = int(input("Enter a number: "))

if num_1 == 20 or num_2 == 20 or num_1+num_2 == 20:
    print(True)

else:
    print(False)
```
